# Seedance prompts — SpeechLyt Hero 60s

6 сцен по ~8.5 сек каждая. Cinematic tech aesthetic, синие/cyan акценты (брендинг SpeechLyt). Все промпты на английском (Seedance работает лучше).

Стиль на все сцены: **cinematic, shallow depth of field, 35mm lens, soft natural lighting, blue/cyan color grade, modern minimalist Belarusian office aesthetic, 4K quality**.

Камера: smooth dolly / slight push-in. Без резких движений.

---

## Scene 1 — `scene_1_silent_office` (8 сек)

**Соответствует voiceover:** «Каждый день ваши операторы делают сотни звонков. Но проверяете вы только пять процентов.»

**Prompt:**
> A modern open-plan call center in Minsk. Wide shot from above. Twenty operators at sleek glass desks, headsets on, talking calmly into microphones. Soft daylight from floor-to-ceiling windows. The camera slowly pushes forward through the rows. One supervisor stands behind a glass wall, looking helpless at a small laptop screen showing only 5 highlighted call rows out of 200. Cinematic, blue-cyan grade, shallow depth of field, 35mm, 4K.

**Negative:** chaotic, neon, cyberpunk, anime, cartoon, text overlays.

---

## Scene 2 — `scene_2_lost_money` (8 сек)

**Соответствует voiceover:** «Где-то теряете клиента. Где-то нарушают скрипт. Где-то оператору нужна помощь...»

**Prompt:**
> Close-up on an operator's face — frustrated, biting lip, looking at a CRM screen showing a deal moving from "Negotiation" to "Lost". Phone is pressed to ear. Background blurred but suggests open office. Light flickers from screen onto her face. Then quick cut: another operator stuttering on a sales script, pages of paper falling. Then: supervisor reading a complaint email three days late, hand on forehead. Three quick beats, each 2.5 sec. Cinematic, dramatic but restrained, blue-cyan grade.

**Negative:** angry shouting, blood, cartoon, exaggerated faces.

## Scene 3 — `scene_3_speechlyt_appears` (10 сек)

**Соответствует voiceover:** «SpeechLyt анализирует сто процентов ваших звонков. На вашем сервере. Без OpenAI. С оплатой в белорусских рублях.»

**Prompt:**
> Smooth transition: dark screen, then a sleek dashboard interface materializes — clean white-on-dark UI with blue accents. The dashboard shows 100% of calls being analyzed in real time, with waveforms, KPI tiles (AHT, CSAT, FCR), and a sentiment heatmap. Camera slowly pulls back to reveal the dashboard is on a server rack screen, in a server room with rows of GPUs. A small Belarusian flag pin sits on the rack. Cool blue lighting, high-tech aesthetic, cinematic.

**Negative:** generic stock footage, cartoon, low-res, watermarks, sci-fi laser effects.

---

## Scene 4 — `scene_4_features_tour` (12 сек)

**Соответствует voiceover:** «Whisper-транскрипция, AI-комплаенс скриптов, эмоции клиентов, авто-QA на 80%. Подсказки оператору в реальном времени.»

**Prompt:**
> Fast-paced UI montage. Five quick beats, each 2.4 sec:
> (1) Live transcription appearing word-by-word with speaker labels Operator/Client.
> (2) Script compliance checklist auto-checking each item green as the conversation progresses.
> (3) Emotion timeline — green/yellow/red ribbon following customer sentiment.
> (4) QA scorecard auto-filling 80% of fields with AI-generated values.
> (5) Real-time prompt popup on operator screen: "Mention the warranty period".
> All in same dashboard style — dark UI, blue accents, sharp typography. Smooth transitions between beats. Cinematic, modern, 4K.

**Negative:** stock UI templates, cartoon, anime UI, cluttered design.

## Scene 5 — `scene_5_proof_clients` (10 сек)

**Соответствует voiceover:** «Дана Холдинг. Системно значимый банк. Телеком-оператор. Все растут на 15–25 % качества за квартал.»

**Prompt:**
> Three-panel split screen, like a documentary. Each panel shows a different Belarusian business setting:
> (1) Дана Холдинг — modern shopping mall (TRC) with people walking, professional-looking woman in business suit smiling at her phone showing positive metrics.
> (2) Bank — marble lobby, sharp business attire, customer at desk, phone consultation with smile.
> (3) Telecom — call center floor with operator using headset, screen visible showing real-time AI prompts, looking confident.
> Slow pan across panels. Subtle KPI stats float in corners (+22%, +18%, +27%). Cinematic, warm but professional, blue-cyan grade.

**Negative:** logos of real companies (just suggest setting), cartoon, exaggerated diversity stereotypes.

## Scene 6 — `scene_6_cta_offer` (8 сек)

**Соответствует voiceover:** «Аудит вашего контакт-центра — за 1 500 белорусских рублей. Сегодня.»

**Prompt:**
> Slow push-in on a clean dashboard reveal: "Аудит контакт-центра — 1 500 BYN — 7 дней". Below it, three guarantee chips light up one by one: "Гарантия 90 дней", "Резидент ПВТ", "Без OpenAI". Background is the operator-floor of scene 1, but now bright and busy with smiling operators. Camera ends on the call-to-action button "Заказать аудит" pulsing softly. Cinematic, hopeful, blue-cyan grade.

**Negative:** generic CTA buttons, comic-sans, neon, cyberpunk overlays.
